You Speak - We Listen - Casual Day Monday

Yesterday @ 1:44:47 / Mrs E McEwen

The SYNERGY team has gained approval from the School Council to run a school casual day on Monday Week 9.

As a school we have identified that we would like to support the schools affected by the recent bushfires. What this means is that all money raised through the casual day will be donated to the schools in need.

If you would like to participate in the casual day on Monday,

please ensure that you bring a gold coin donation.

Teachers and SYNERGY members will collect donations during Connect Group on Monday morning.

If you sign in late, please leave your money at Student Services.

In order to get the casual day approved School Council have proposed the following dress code. Please adhere to the following:

  • Shoulders to be covered at all times
  • No tops with midriff or plunging necklines to be worn
  • Hemlines for shorts, skirts and dresses no shorter mid-thigh
  • Closed in shoes must be worn (sports shoes must be worn in the gym)
  • Full PE uniform STILL required for Lifestyle Choices
  • No offensive logos or language
  • No see-through garments
